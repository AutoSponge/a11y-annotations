Mouse Controls

  • Annotation elements and Legend are draggable. Left-click to "grab" the element.
  • Holding shift⇧ and dragging down/right will grow.
  • Holding shift⇧ and dragging up/left will shrink.

Keyboard Commands

Annotations are in tab order but are always after all body content. You can use tab and shift⇧ + tab to move between them. The legend should always be the last thing in tab order. While focused on an annotation, you can save using ctrl⌃ + shift⇧ + s and toggle the legend with ctrl⌃ + shift⇧ + l
